Example Results

On this site, you can see a possible output of the application. On the upper right part, the different basemaps and the classification results can be selected. This result shows the difference in the classification result for a calculation with and without additonal LiDAR data. When you compare the two maps, it is easy to see that especially trees and shrubs are often confused when only spectral imagery is used. The vegetation stability shows, in which areas the chages takes place and where both calculation have the same result.
